FIFA world ranking

The FIFA World Ranking is a ranking system for the national teams in football, which is currently led by Belgium. The teams of the member nations of FIFA association, are ranked based on their games played. The rankings were introduced in December 1992, and eight teams have held the top position, of which Brazil have spent the longest ranked first.

the best team - Belgium

Points: 1765

The Belgium national football team officially represents Belgium in men's international football since 1904. The squad is under the global jurisdiction of FIFA assosiation and is governed in Europe by UEFA assosiation. Periods of regular Belgian representation at the highest international level, from 1920 to 1938, from 1982 to 2002 and again from 2014 onwards, have alternated with mostly unsuccessful qualification rounds. Most of Belgium's home matches are played at the King Baudouin Stadium in Brussels.
(Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/FIFA_World_Rankings)

The France national football team represents France in men's international football and is controlled by the French Football Federation. The team's colours are blue, white and red. France are colloquially known as The Blues. They are the reigning world champions having won the most recent World Cup final in 2018.
